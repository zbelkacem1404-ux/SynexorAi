⚠ Deviation Handling

When a booking doesn't match its route plan (wrong pickup day, different carrier, equipment mismatch), SynexorAI automatically flags it, requires a justification from the planner, and routes it to the Approval Board for supervisor review.

💰 Spot Request Flow

No matching route plan? The system creates a spot request, allows you to request quotes from multiple forwarders, compare pricing and transit times side-by-side, then assign the best option with one click.
